All About Apollo

We adopted Apollo in September 2020 from the Lagotto Senones kennel based on the Somerset coast. Apollo is the youngest of his litter and we immediately fell in love with his goofy charm. When we selected Apollo, the breeder did say that that he has a super personality, I didn't understand how he knew that based on Apollo's 8 weeks of life, but now I know he was right. He has been with us since and has truly become a part of our family.

Apollo is a Lagotto Romagnolo, an Italian gundog breed of duck retrievers now famous for truffle hunting. He is a mix between the sweetest puppy that loves to cuddle and a boy with so much attitude that I can't help but laugh, even when he's barking at me or running from me when he's in trouble.

We live in south-west London, surrounded by beautiful parks and green space for lots of fun walks.

Learn about the Lagotto Romangnolo Breed

The Lagotto Romagnolo is an ancient breed of duck retriever from the Romagna sub-region of Italy. With the reclamation of the marshes, the Lagotto’s phenomenal nose meant that this breed of duck retriever was able to transition to a new role of truffle hunting.
